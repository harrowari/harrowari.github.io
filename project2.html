Trend prediction in the music industry has changed since the inception of social media and with it, an abundance of data about listener’s preferences. Sentiment analysis can provide statistical information about such preferences and when combined with the overall volume of tweets, predict the future success of a song or album. At present, trend prediction studies using twitter data give every tweet equal weight, irrespective of the tweeter’s following. However, social media influencers play an important role in the marketing ecosystem given their large followings and reach. As a result, it could be suggested that not all tweets are of equal weight in regards to their influence on a song or album’s promotion. If this is the case, current prediction algorithms would be missing a key variable which could augment their accuracy.
